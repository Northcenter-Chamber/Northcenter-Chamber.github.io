September 18th & 19th

Why Homecoming?

After over a year inside, we're throwing a Homecoming celebration showcasing the best of Northcenter! Keep September 18th & 19th open for a weekend of local food & drink, live music and community in Northcenter Town Square! Stay tuned to this website and our social media channels for more details in the weeks ahead.

Togetherness

Homecoming events are all about coming together as a community, connecting with old friends and making new ones. At Northcenter Homecoming, we hope you’ll call up an old friend you lost touch with over the past year and invite them out for a catch-up, or meet a new neighbor you haven’t talked to before.

Pride

High schools and colleges all over the country celebrate homecoming as a source of school pride. Our Homecoming is focused on neighborhood pride, showcasing the best of Northcenter—our talented local musicians, our delicious restaurants, our award-winning craft breweries and, most of all, our wonderful neighbors.

Celebration

We’ve spent the past year and a half inside, but that doesn’t mean we were disconnected. Our neighbors and local businesses worked together to support and take care of one another in an uncertain time. Now that we’re able to be safely out and about again, we want to honor you, our neighbors, and treat you to a fun fall celebratiion.
